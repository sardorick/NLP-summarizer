Problem Definition

One of the most difficult tasks in research is reading and reviewing long list of published scientific papers. This task can be daunting as readers spend many hours reading most papers only to realize that the paper is unrelated to the topic of interest. This is also true of people who read several texts, among them are books, blogs, online information, and others. The pain of going through all these can be eased by finding a model that summarizers the content of any document, where the user will only read the summary of the content.

2. Summarize a pdf document once uploaded.
3. Summarize a website document just by providing the url-link
